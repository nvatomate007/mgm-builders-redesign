# MGM Builders — Design Direction

## Chosen Direction: Warm Craftsman Authority

**Design Movement:** Warm Craftsman Authority — premium residential contractor meets trusted local institution.

**Core Principles:**
1. Warmth over coldness — cream, slate, and deep red ground the brand in craftsmanship, not tech
2. Asymmetric authority — left-anchored hero with right-side form; never centered-only layouts
3. Conversion clarity — every section has one job; no decorative noise
4. Mobile-first CTA persistence — phone number and "Get a Free Quote" always within thumb reach

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: MGM Red `#C41E3A` — the brand's established identity, used for CTAs and accents only
- Background: Off-white `#FAFAF8` — warm, not clinical
- Surface: Slate `#1C2B3A` — deep navy-slate for headers and footers; authoritative, not dark-mode
- Text: Charcoal `#2D2D2D` — readable, warm
- Accent: Warm amber `#D4A853` — used sparingly for trust badges and star ratings

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Hero: Full-bleed finished-home image (right), left-anchored headline + form overlay
- Services: 2×3 card grid with icon, title, one-line description — no carousels
- About: Split layout — left text, right blueprint image
- Testimonials: Horizontal scroll on mobile, 3-column on desktop
- CTA Banner: Full-width red band, white text, single button
- Footer: Dark slate, three columns

**Signature Elements:**
1. Red left-border accent on section headings (4px solid MGM Red)
2. Blueprint image as the "before" visual in the About section
3. Sticky mobile bottom bar with phone number + quote button

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states on service cards: subtle lift shadow + red left border reveal
- Form fields: clean, no-frills with clear labels
- Scroll: smooth, no parallax — fast load is the priority

**Animation:**
- Entrance: fade-up on section headers (150ms, no delay stacking)
- No looping animations, no particle effects, no scroll-jacking
- Hover transitions: 200ms ease

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif) — headlines and section titles; weight 700
- Body: Source Sans 3 (sans-serif) — body copy, form labels, nav; weight 400/600
- Mono accent: none
- Scale: 48px hero → 32px section → 20px card → 16px body → 14px caption
